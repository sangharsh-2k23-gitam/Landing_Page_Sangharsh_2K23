About Sangharsh 6.0

Sangharsh is an annual outdoor tech festival focused on bringing top industry experts. GITAM Mechanical Department is back and invites you to join Sangharsh Tech Fest 6.0 where the whole STEM ecosystem gathers to exhibit their teams and innovations and to jointly celebrate the ideal locale for igniting your interest in technology.

Once in Lifetime Experience

Getting to know engineering transforms young minds into skilled and confident engineers, and to achieve this, an annual technical festival called Sangharsh will be organized. The participants are provided a platform to showcase their various talents. Sangharsh, over the years has matured to be acknowledged as one of the biggest confluence for the mechanical students, academicians and industry in the region.
